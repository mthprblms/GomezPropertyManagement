Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our property management services

How much do you charge for property management?

Our property management fees are competitive and transparent. We typically charge a percentage of the monthly rent collected, which covers all our comprehensive services including tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance coordination, and financial reporting. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific property needs.

How do you screen potential tenants?

We conduct rigorous tenant screening that includes comprehensive credit checks, criminal background checks, employment verification, rental history verification, and income verification. Our multi-step process ensures we place only qualified, reliable tenants in your property, protecting your investment and reducing turnover.

How long does it take to find a tenant?

The rental market in Clarksville and Middle Tennessee is dynamic. On average, we can place a qualified tenant within 2-4 weeks of listing your property. Our professional photography, strategic pricing, and marketing across multiple platforms help minimize vacancy time and maximize your rental income.

Do you handle maintenance requests?

Absolutely! We coordinate all maintenance requests through our 24/7 emergency hotline and trusted network of licensed vendors. We handle everything from routine maintenance to emergency repairs, ensuring quick response times and quality work at competitive prices. You'll receive detailed reports on all maintenance activities.

When do I get my rental income?

We provide monthly rental income distributions, typically processed within 3-5 business days after rent is collected. You'll receive detailed monthly statements showing all income and expenses, making tax time easier. We also provide year-end 1099 forms for your convenience.
